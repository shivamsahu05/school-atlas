# SAMS ATLAS Platform - Teacher User Manual

## Introduction

### Welcome to SAMS ATLAS
SAMS ATLAS is your personal digital teaching assistant. It replaces traditional paper diaries, physical syllabus trackers, and manual mark registers. Everything you need to plan your day, track your students' progress, and monitor your own performance is available in one easy-to-use platform.

### Benefits for Teachers
* **Clarity:** Know exactly what you need to teach today with the Micro Schedule.
* **Simplicity:** Mark syllabus topics as complete with a single click.
* **Transparency:** View your live performance scores and see exactly how you are being evaluated.
* **Convenience:** Apply for leave and view school notices directly from your phone or laptop.

## TEACHER MODULES

### Micro Schedule & Syllabus Tracking
#### Purpose
To help you plan your daily lessons and report your progress to the administration.
#### When To Use
Every single day after you finish teaching a class.
#### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Click **Micro Schedule** from the main menu.
2. Select your Class and Subject.
3. You will see a list of topics planned for the week/month.
4. Once you finish teaching a topic, check the box to mark it as **Completed**.
#### Expected Result
Your progress bar will increase, and your "Syllabus Completion" performance score will improve.
#### Common Mistakes
Forgetting to log in and mark topics as completed. Even if you taught the class, the system will assume you are behind schedule until you click the checkbox.

### Learning Outcomes (LO)
#### Purpose
To evaluate how well individual students grasped the topics you taught.
#### When To Use
At the end of a chapter or weekly learning cycle.
#### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Click **Learning Outcomes**.
2. Select your Class and Subject.
3. You will see a list of your students.
4. For each student, select their understanding level: **Approaching** (struggling), **Meeting** (average), or **Exceeding** (excellent).
5. Click **Submit**.
#### Expected Result
These scores are sent to the Principal for final approval.

### Performance
#### Purpose
To view your objective appraisal scores calculated by the system.
#### When To Use
End of the month.
#### Step-by-Step Instructions
1. Click **Performance**.
2. View your breakdown: Syllabus Completion (15%), LO Achievement (15%), Classroom Observation (25%), Language (15%), Participation (10%), Other (20%).
3. Your **Overall Score** is calculated automatically based on these metrics.
#### Important Notes
If your Syllabus Completion score is 0%, it means you have not been using the Micro Schedule module to mark your topics as complete.

## TEACHER DAILY WORKFLOW (SOPs)

### Daily Teaching Activities
1. Log in to SAMS ATLAS in the morning.
2. Check your **Time Table** for the day's schedule.
3. Check **Events & Notices** for urgent announcements.
4. After classes conclude, open the **Micro Schedule** and mark today's taught topics as "Completed".

### Weekly Planning Activities
1. Review the **Syllabus Tracking** report to see if you are ahead or behind the monthly plan.
2. Submit **Learning Outcomes** for the topics completed that week.

### Assessment Activities
1. After taking a test or collecting homework, open **Marks Entry**.
2. Input the grades for all students before the deadline.

### Leave Request Process
1. Do not use WhatsApp or paper for official leaves. 
2. Submit your application exclusively through the **Leave Management** module at least 48 hours in advance (unless it is an emergency).

## TEACHER FAQ

**Q: I am unable to login. What should I do?**
A: Check that your email is spelled correctly and caps lock is off. If it still fails, ask the Admin to reset your password.

**Q: Why are my classes or subjects missing from the dropdown menu?**
A: The system only shows classes assigned to you by the Principal. If something is missing, contact the Admin and ask them to update your profile in the Teacher Directory.

**Q: Why is my Performance Score so low?**
A: Performance is calculated automatically. If you forget to update your Micro Schedule, your Syllabus score will drop. Ensure you log in daily and record your progress accurately.

**Q: I made a mistake in Marks Entry. Can I change it?**
A: Yes, you can re-enter the marks and click Save again, provided the Admin has not locked the grading period.

**Q: How do I know if my leave is approved?**
A: Go to the **Leave Management** module. The status will change from "Pending" to either "Approved" or "Rejected". You may also see a notification on your Dashboard.
